Innovation

Our goal is to be the first undergraduate group of students to design a 3D printed, low cost, and fully Autonomous Underwater Vehicle (AUV) which will allow for the implementation of several sensor types for oceanographic and climate change research.


Research

Oceanographic research, especially in today’s climate, is very important, but researchers do not have access to low cost equipment. We are here to change that.


Education

Provide students with experience that will allow them to make positive change and become tomorrow’s innovators and change-makers.
